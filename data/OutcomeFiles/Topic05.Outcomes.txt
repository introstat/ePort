Topic 05: Summarizing a Quantitative Variable between Groups

After studying this topic, students should be able to:

A. Choose suitable displays for comparing the distributions of a quantitative variable for two or more groups.
B. Identify the components of an outlier boxplot.
C. Compare and contrast the distributions of a quantitative variable for two or more groups using boxplots or histograms.
D. Use the 5-number summary for a quantitative variable to determine potential outliers in the boxplot.
E. Use boxplots and histograms to identify outliers for quantitative variables.
