Topic 01: Data


After studying this topic, students should be able to:

A. Identify the context of the data by answering the 5 W questions (who, what, where, when, why, and how).
B. Identify the “who” and “what” data context questions from the structure of the data table. 
C. Determine the structure of the data table based on given information.
D. Determine whether variables are quantitative or categorical variables.
E. Identify units for a quantitative variable.
F. Identify categories for a categorical variable.
G. Distinguish between population and sample in a given scenario.
